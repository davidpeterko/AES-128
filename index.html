Ko Encryption

Who was it for?

This was a project for myself for being able to encrypt sensitive data.

What was it?

It is an encryption algorithm based on AES 256. It has the underlying components of AES; the diffusion and confusion, but with another twist to try and make AES even stronger than it already is.

When was it?

I recently started working on this project, within the last 2 months.

Where was this?

I have been working on this back and forth between my home and the computer lab on the campus of the University of Texas.

Why was it done?

Test my Java programming skills and knowledge of cryptography to try and create a better encryption scheme.

How was it done?

By writing an AES 256 implementation by myself in Java, I slowly picked apart different parts of the AES that I wanted to modify for myself. I do not want to give away the specifics because that would defeat the purpose of having an encryption if they would reverse engineer the decryption. But heres a hint: it modifies the 4 components of AES (addRoundKeys, mixColumns, etc) and it may or may not try and apply transposition and substitution at unspecified places! Good luck!
